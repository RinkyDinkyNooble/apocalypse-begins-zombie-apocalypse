**Apocalypse Begins [Zombie Apocalypse]** is a modern zombie apocalypse survival modpack. It includes a handcrafted modern city, specialized survival mechanics, and a growing zombie threat that escalates over time. The goal of the modpack is to **craft the vaccine** & survive **100 Days**. It is designed for the **hardcore** gamemode, but can be enjoyed casually too.

---

## ✨ Features

The following are some of the features this modpack includes, *but isn't limited to*:

### ⚔️ Combat & Apocalypse

- **Superb Warfare** - Firearms, melee weapons, armor, turrets, and missiles.
- **Better Combat** - Melee weapon combat overhaul.
- **The Hordes** - Hordes strike each player periodically, increasing in difficulty each time.
- **Improved Mobs** - Increased mob intelligence. Zombies will learn to mine blocks, steal items, and use off-hand items such as ender pearls and lava buckets. Zombies come in all shapes and sizes, with increasing stats scaling with difficulty.
- **Artifacts** - Loot you can find throughout the city that usually gives you a massive buff.

### 🌡️ Survival Mechanics

- **First Aid** - Limb-based health system where you manually bandage wounded areas to heal.
- **Diet** - Managing to keep a diet above 50% for all categories gives you regeneration. If any falls below 5%, you get the hunger status effect. This requires you to eat a variety of food.
- **Always Eat** - You can eat past full hunger bars in order to meet nutritional standards.
- **Tough as Nails** - Thirst and temperature are necessary components to manage. Finding, purifying, and storing drinkable water will be a new challenge. Temperature will affect where you can go at any time, forcing you to build bases and technology to keep you at room temperature.
- **Hardcore Revival** - Revive your downed teammates.
- **Parcool** - Navigate the world using parkour to outrun your enemies and reach what they can't quicker.
- **Hardcore Lite** - If you die, you lose a heart (in non-hardcore gamemode).

### 🤖 Technology & Progression

- **Create** - Using stress to automate farms, generate materials, and build secure contraptions to protect yourself from the zombies.
- **Create New Age** - Introduces electricity generation and nuclear reactors to power everything from vehicles to storage systems
- **Create Slice & Dice** - Integrates with Farmer's Delight to allow automation of managing your diet.
- **Pufferfish's Skills** - Use skill points to upgrade your attributes like max health to increase your survivability. Earn skill points by slaying mobs.

### 👥 Multiplayer & Immersion

- **Simple Voice Chat** - Play and chat with friends using proximity voice chat.
- **What Are They Up To** - See your friend's inventories and UI's when they are accessing it.
- **Presence Footsteps** - Use your hearing to locate zombie threats approaching you.
- **Open Parties & Claims** - Claim chunks, forceload your contraptions, and build a secure base with your team.

### 🌎 World & Exploration

- **Xaero's World Map** - Mark waypoints to your base or points of interest and see your teammates on the map.
- **Lost Cities** - Custom modern city generation with a plethora of handpicked loot. Navigated using Superb Warfare ground vehicles along highways and roads.
- **Serene Seasons** - Forces you to stockpile food for winter and prepare a warm base as farming in winter is much harder and colder.
- **Nature's Compass** - Find any biome you want using a special compass.

### 📦 Storage & Quality of Life

- **Sophisticated Backpacks** - Carry massive amounts of loot at once as you explore, with no limit to how many backpacks you can have.
- **Refined Storage** - Set up a mass storage system at your base with automated appendages and auto-sorted grid views.
- **Client Sort** - Sort your inventory & chests. Transfer items between inventories with one click.
- **XP Tome** - Store your xp for scenarios where you may lose it.
- **Corpse Mod** - Collect your items after death with one click.

## ⚡ Optimization & Performance

This modpack was **designed with performance** in mind *(especially since the world is in a memory chip shortage)*. It keeps the use of heavy mods low and also sticks to **default minecraft terrain** generation to massively boost performance that would otherwise be lost. 

### DRAM Recommendations

- *Playable* - `2048 MB` *(using Distant Horizons or shaders is **NOT** recommended)*
- **Recommended** - **`4096 MB`** (can use Distant Horizons and shaders)
- *Beautiful Graphics* - `4096 MB` - `8192 MB` *(can use far Distant Horizons render distances at roughly 256+)*

### VRAM Recommendations

- *Playable* - Integrated Graphics *(using Distant Horizons or shaders is **NOT** recommended)*
- **Recommended** - **`2048 MB`** - **`6144 MB`** (can use Distant Horizons and shaders)
- *Beautiful Graphics* - `8192 MB`+ *(can use far Distant Horizons render distances and high-end profiles for shaders)*

### ⚠️ WARNINGS

- Do **NOT** use **Distant Horizons** for chunk generation. It is disabled by default. Use **Chunky** instead.
- Do **NOT** destroy **Create: New Age** blocks with explosions from **Superb Warfare**.

### 🪲 Known Bugs & Glitches

This modpack does contain a few bugs. Bugs are patched according to their severity (game breaking or not). Some trivial bugs *may* not be addressed (if I'm too incompetent to do so 💀). Sometimes bugs can be solved by updated corresponding mods to newer versions, but is often delayed / un-doable due to compatibility or dependency issues. 

🟢 Solved / Mitigated <br>
🟡 Unsolved Minor Bug <br>
🟠 Unsolved Moderate Bug <br>
🔴 Unsolved Critical Bug (Game Crashing / Unplayable) <br>

1. 🟡 Certain block entities fail to render until interacted with (e.g., beds, Create items). This bug is **NOT** game breaking or significant, but it is noticeable.
2. 🟢 Using chunk generation from distant horizons breaks something (it may be the city generation, not certain, but resolved using Chunky). 
3. 🟡 Random floating mobs in an idle state (non-aggressive or dead). Usually solved by restarting the game / server.
4. 🔴 Destroying Create: New Age blocks with explosions from Superb Warfare causes the game / server to crash. 
5. 🟠 Some players are not capable of being successfully revived when downed.
6. 🟡 Using shaders while rendering Superb Warfare entities causes a performance dip, noticeable on lower end PCs.
7. 🟡 Backpacks have a GUI formatting mistake where upgrade slots go.
